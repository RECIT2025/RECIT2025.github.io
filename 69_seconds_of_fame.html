69 Seconds of Fame

Description

In this event, Participants are supposed to showcase their talents in the given time constraint. The talents can be of any type such as music, comedy, dance, acting, magic.

Instructions and Rules

  1. 1-3 members(s) per team.
  2. Partcipants will be given 69 seconds to showcase their talents.
  3. The main goal is to grab the judge's attention and impress them.
  4. The winner would be awarded with cash prizes.
  5. Any kind of offensive or controversial content will lead to immediate disqualification.
